About

The Rent Management System helps landlord to collect rents from tenants with ease and maintain their properties effectively.

  • Landlord can create property and add units and tenants to it.
  • Landlord can see rent payments of each unit and pending payments without hassle.
  • Landlord and Tenant both got the rental payments as record for future use like Tax filing.

Landlord can keep track of issues posted by tenants. Effectively resolving the property issues. Tenants can be able to raise complaint to the Landlord on the property issues. Landlords can able to access all details for their property; whereas tenants are permitted to view their respective property only.
